About

Welcome to Knowing Blue Jays, a site created out of a deep passion for one of nature’s most vibrant and captivating birds — the Blue Jay. Founded in 2024 by a lifelong bird enthusiast, this space was born from countless hours spent observing these brilliant creatures in backyards, forests, and parks. From their striking blue feathers to their intelligent, curious behavior, Blue Jays have always held a special place in our hearts.

Our mission is to share that love with you, offering a space where fellow Blue Jay admirers can learn, appreciate, and celebrate these fascinating birds. Whether you’re here for tips on attracting Blue Jays to your yard, discovering their unique traits, or simply to marvel at stunning photography, we hope this site inspires your own appreciation for these feathered wonders.

Thank you for joining us in celebrating the beauty and wonder of Blue Jays!
